## 8. Your Rights (GDPR & PDPA)

You have the following rights:

### 8.1 Right to Access
- **View your data**: Access all your wisdom submissions and consent records
- **Export your data**: Download in JSON, Markdown, or CSV format

### 8.2 Right to Rectification
- **Edit submissions**: Update your wisdom stories and metadata
- **Correct errors**: Fix any inaccurate information

### 8.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Delete submissions**: Remove individual wisdom entries
- **Delete account**: Permanently delete your account and all data
- **Timeline**: Deletion completed within 72 hours

### 8.4 Right to Data Portability
- **Export formats**: JSON (for personal LLM training), Markdown, CSV
- **Bulk download**: Download all your submissions at once

### 8.5 Right to Withdraw Consent
- **Revoke AI training consent**: Stop your data from being used for AI training
- **Change Z-Protocol tier**: Adjust your consent level at any time
- **Immediate effect**: Withdrawal processed within 24 hours

### 8.6 Right to Object
- **Object to processing**: Request we stop processing your data for specific purposes
- **Opt-out of analytics**: Disable anonymized analytics tracking

### 8.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint
If you believe we violated your privacy rights, you can file a complaint with:

- **EU**: Your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)
- **Malaysia**: Personal Data Protection Department (JPDP)
- **Singapore**: Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC)
- **California**: California Attorney General's Office

## 9. Z-Protocol v2.0 Framework

YSenseAI operates under the **Z-Protocol v2.0**, our comprehensive ethical framework for AI attribution and cultural protection.

### 9.1 Five-Tier Classification System

**TIER 1: PUBLIC** (15% revenue sharing)
- General reflections, non-sensitive content
- Standard privacy compliance
- Basic consent requirements

**TIER 2: PERSONAL** (20% revenue sharing)
- Individual experiences, family references
- Enhanced privacy protections
- Explicit opt-in required

**TIER 3: CULTURAL** (25% + community fund)
- Traditional knowledge, cultural practices
- Community attribution required
- Benefit sharing with cultural communities

**TIER 4: SACRED** (30% + mandatory fund)
- Religious, spiritual content
- Restricted access protocols
- Special community approval required

**TIER 5: THERAPEUTIC** (25% + research fund)
- Mental health, trauma content
- Medical ethics oversight
- Professional validation required

### 9.2 Consent Types

You control these consent types independently:

1. **Basic Platform Use** (Required)
   - Account creation and platform functionality
   - Cannot be withdrawn (account deletion required)

2. **Wisdom Storage** (Required for submissions)
   - Store your wisdom in our database
   - Can be withdrawn by deleting submissions

3. **AI Training Contribution** (Optional, Compensated)
   - Use your anonymized content for AI training
   - Revenue sharing: 15-30% based on tier
   - Fully withdrawable at any time

4. **Community Sharing** (Optional)
   - Share reflections within YSense community
   - Fully withdrawable

5. **Cultural Knowledge Sharing** (Optional, for Cultural/Sacred tiers)
   - Share with cultural communities
   - Requires community approval
   - Benefit sharing with communities

6. **Research Participation** (Optional)
   - Academic research use
   - Anonymized and aggregated
   - Fully withdrawable
